## CUTE (CU Trading Emporium)

Welcome to CUTE, the CU Trading Emporium! This Python-based portfolio manager is designed to empower CU Quant's trading strategies, providing a robust platform for managing and optimizing your investment portfolios. Whether you're a seasoned quantitative analyst or a novice trader, CUTE is here to streamline your portfolio management process.

### Features

#### 1. Strategy Integration
Easily integrate and manage multiple trading strategies within a unified platform. CUTE supports the seamless implementation of CU Quant's diverse range of trading algorithms.

#### 2. Portfolio Tracking
Effortlessly track the performance of your portfolios in real-time. CUTE provides comprehensive insights into key metrics, allowing you to make data-driven decisions and optimize your investment strategies.

#### 3. Risk Management (In Development)
Mitigate risks effectively with CUTE's built-in risk management tools. Set risk thresholds, monitor exposure, and receive alerts to ensure your portfolios stay within predefined risk parameters.

#### 4. Customization 
Tailor CUTE to fit your specific needs. The program supports customization of parameters, allowing you to adapt the platform to the unique requirements of CU Quant's trading strategies.

#### 5. Backtesting (In Development)
Test your trading strategies against historical data to evaluate their performance and identify areas for improvement. CUTE's backtesting capabilities help refine your algorithms and enhance overall strategy effectiveness.

#### 6. User-Friendly Interface (In Development)
CUTE boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it accessible to both experienced traders and those new to quantitative analysis. The platform's design prioritizes ease of use without compromising on functionality.
